WeatherVoo

Purpose

The main purpose of Polaris Weather, known as PolarisWeather.com, will be to provide users with a web and mobile friendly interface to find weather for their city in Idaho. Its objective will be fulfilled by having consistent weather updates, and a responsive interface through which users can use across devices.

Domain Name

The domain name will be PolarisWeather.com.

Site Logo

The Polaris Weather site logo will be a sun on the North Star, also known as Polaris.
